My roadmap is organized by capabilities instead of tools:

1. Networking and distributed systems fundamentals.
2. CI/CD and release automation.
3. Observability: metrics, logs, traces, and alerting.
4. Container operations and orchestration.
5. Reliability engineering and incident management.

Each project in this site should demonstrate progress across at least one capability.
